Who am I?

Hey there! I'm Meredith—a dynamic individual with a passion for community, creativity, and coffee. As a dedicated leader at my local church, I foster connections and create meaningful experiences within our small group. My commitment to fostering a sense of belonging extends beyond the spiritual realm; by day, I channel my energy as a skilled barista, crafting delicious beverages that not only satisfy taste buds but also create moments of joy for each customer.

Film is my canvas for inspiration, and I find endless fascination in its ability to convey emotions, tell stories, and spark conversations. This love for the cinematic arts fuels my own creativity, allowing me to approach both work and life with a unique perspective.

Welcome to a space where passion meets purpose, and where every interaction is an opportunity to create something beautiful. Join me in this journey of community, creativity, and, of course, a good cup of coffee.
